Take Care of your Health

Let's Be Aware

Never neglect your physical as well as your mental health. Many people, who tend to avoid precautionary measures, find themselves in great difficulty in the long run. Whenever you are suffering from medical issues, consult a doctor and take the required medicines. Besides, it is essential to eat a balanced diet, follow a healthy routine and exercise regularly. You can succeed in your life if you are healthy, but you will always find it difficult to work when you are suffering from any kind of illness. However, you should never panic in an adverse situation as it can affect your mental health. If you feel monotonous or depressed, listen to songs, do meditation or read good news to feel relaxed and have a positive mindset.
Stay safe and healthy!



Types of Diseases

Non-Communicable Diseases

Nutritional Deficiency Diseases

These diseases are caused due to the deficiency of proteins, carbohydrates, vitamins and minerals.
Examples of such diseases are Kwashiorkar, Marasmus, Beriberi, Scury, Rickets, Night Blindness, Haemorrhage, Goitre, Anaemia.
Eat food items rich in iron, iodine, calcium, phosphorus, vitamin A, B, C, D, E and K for preventing such diseases.

Metabolic Diseases

These diseases are caused due to the malfunctioning of your body organs or dysfunctioning of hormonal secretion.
An example of a metabolic disease is Diabetes To know about the preventive measures for diabetes, check your glucose level and follow the necessary tips (click here).

Communicable Diseases

Diseases Caused by Virus

Common cold, Influenza, Measles, Chickenpox, Polio, Rabies, AIDS, Swine flu

Diseases Caused by Bacteria

Tuberculosis, Whooping Cough, Tetanus, Typhoid, Diptheria, Pneumonia, Cholera

Diseases Caused by Protozoa

Malaria, Sleeping sickness, Dysentery

Diseases Caused by Fungi

Ringworm, Athlete's Foot, Food Poisoning
